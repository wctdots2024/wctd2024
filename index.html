My page about hockey!

I started playing hockey on roller blades. Then I learned to ice skate and changed to ice hockey. I played as a goaltender for many years.

You miss 100% of the shots you don't take -Wayne Gretzky

Today I coach a girls team named the Mighty Moose.

The most fundamental skill to playing ice hockey is skating!

Equipment

To start playing you need the following equipment:

  • Hockey stick
  • Hockey puck
  • Hockey skates
  • Hockey helmet
  • Hockey gloves
  • Hockey pants
  • Hockey shorts
  • Hockey socks
  • Hockey goal
